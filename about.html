Camp Director: Quentin Coleman

Quentin “QC” Coleman is a nationally ranked high school basketball player from Principia High School in St. Louis. Known for his scoring ability, discipline, and leadership, QC competes at the highest levels of youth and high school basketball in the country.

This camp was created to give St. Louis youth access to elite instruction, mindset training, and exposure to what high-level basketball demands.
